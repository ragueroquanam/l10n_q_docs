This module provides additional purchase functionality extending the base Purchase Q features with extra purchase management capabilities, enhanced workflows, and additional purchase operations.

## Features

* **Enhanced Purchase Management**: Additional purchase management capabilities beyond standard functionality
* **Extended Purchase Workflows**: Advanced workflow enhancements and purchase process improvements
* **Additional Purchase Operations**: Support for additional purchase operations and processing options
* **Enhanced Purchase Integration**: Improved integration with inventory, accounting, and supplier management

## Dependencies

* `purchase`: Core purchase functionality
