**Version:** 18.0.1.0.0  
**Odoo Version:** 18.0

This module provides integration between cash management and POS systems, enabling seamless cash flow tracking, POS-to-cash synchronization, and unified cash management across POS and back-office operations.

## Features

* **Cash-POS Integration**: Direct integration between cash management and POS systems
* **Cash Flow Synchronization**: Real-time synchronization of cash movements between POS and cash management
* **Unified Cash Tracking**: Consolidated cash tracking across POS operations and cash management sessions
* **Cross-System Reconciliation**: Advanced reconciliation between POS cash operations and cash management records

## Dependencies

* `base`: Core Odoo functionality
